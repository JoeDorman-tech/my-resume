Publications

  • Kinematic Modelling of a Three-Axis Articulated Robotic Arm
    Developed and validated the kinematic model of a three-degree-of-freedom articulated robotic arm for industrial applications. The design utilized CAD software, and forward and inverse kinematics were derived analytically using the Denavit-Hartenberg convention. Verified the model's accuracy and precision through MATLAB, RoboAnalyzer, and Fusion 360, demonstrating the arm’s ability to reach any position in its workspace with four possible configurations.
    Link: https://doi.org/10.1007/978-981-97-7094-6_25
  • FNN-Based Inverse Kinematics for Efficient Trajectory Planning in Industrial Robots
    Investigated the use of Feedforward Neural Networks (FNNs) for solving task-specific inverse kinematics of industrial robots, aiming to achieve high accuracy and rapid training. The ABB IRB 6700 robot was used as a case study. Performance was evaluated using ME, STD, and RMSE, showing reduced training times and accurate predictions.
    Link: https://doi.org/10.1109/IACIS61494.2024.10721967
  • A Novel Adaptive Levenberg-Marquardt Algorithm for Industrial Robot Inverse Kinematics
    Developed and evaluated an adaptive LM algorithm using the ABB IRB 6700 to optimize convergence speed and computational efficiency. Demonstrated improved performance compared to standard methods at both singular and non-singular poses.
    Link: https://www.routledge.com/Integrated-Technologies...
  • Image Enhancement Techniques in Satellite Imagery using MATLAB and OpenCV
    Implemented a range of image enhancement techniques on satellite images using MATLAB and OpenCV. Performance was analyzed using SNR, PSNR, RMSE, and MAE metrics.
